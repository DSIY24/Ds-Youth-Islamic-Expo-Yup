Art Competition​​

  • •Open to all students (Islamic and Non-Islamic).
  • •This is an individual competition and consists of only one round
  • •The art work should either have a reference to any divine book relating to the theme of the event (i.e. ‘Faith, Family and Community’).
  • •The competition is art in any form: painting, calligraphy, a 2D or 3D model.
  • •The students will be asked to talk about their art work for a duration of 90 seconds.​
  • •While speaking, if a participant starts the speech again (due to some error), then the time will not be recorded again.
  • •It is up to the creativity of the participants to choose the size and dimensions of the paper/material on which they would do the art.
  • •Participants get their materials and assemble it in Dubai Scholars on the day of the competition (13th January 2024).
  • •Ready made projects will be disqualified.
  • •The model must have an A4 size sheet indicating the category, student name and school name clearly.
