Please view the following document containing all the general rules and regulations which need to be strictly adhered to for the competition.

  • •The competition is open for all the schools approved by KHDA and the Ministry of Education of the United Arab Emirates.
  • •The event will be held physically on-site at Dubai Scholars Private School. It is to be only attended by the participating student(s) and a teacher (from the respective school); not by parents.
  • •The competition is open for all the schools approved by KHDA and the Ministry of Education of the United Arab Emirates.
  • •The participants should be a bonafide student of the school.
  • •The age of the participants (and therefore, the age groups) must be calculated as on 29th November 2022.
  • •Participants for the Qirat Recitation Competition sub-category should recite the specified Surah (according to their age category) from memory.
  • •A warning bell will be alarmed after the allotted time. The participants are to not continue after the warning bell has been rung.
  • •All participants for this event will receive individual Certificates of Participation.
  • •The decision of the judges will be final.
  • •As part of External Registration, separate entry forms for boys and girls are available on the ‘Registration’ section on the website, which may be duly completed.
  • •The participants must attend the event in their respective school uniforms.
  • •The Overall Championship Trophy for the year will be awarded to the school achieving the maximum number of overall points (i.e. for both girls and boys combined together across all categories).
  • •Please feel free to contact us for any queries or concerns that you may have

Plase note that lunch will not be provided
