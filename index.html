About The Event

About the event

Welcome to the sixth edition of DS Islamic Youth Expo, organised by our very own Islamic department and sixth form students. A mega inter school competition that enables both Muslim and Non-Muslims to showcase their skills and talent in three individual categories: qirat, art and elocution. Since one of Islam’s core values and morals revolves around family and kinship, this year’s theme for our event is “Family and Community”. In a hadith narrated by Abu Huraira, the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: “He who believes in Allah and the Last Day, let him maintain good relations with kins.” (Sahih al-Bukhari and Muslim). This portrays the importance of kinship in Islam and emphasises that the strength of the Muslim community lies in its unity, support system, and shared values, all grounded in a common faith.

Competitions

Qirat (Qur'an Recitation) Competition

The participants must recite the Surah from the beginning for a duration of 3 minutes.

Sustainable Art Competition​​

Sustainable art blurs the line between science and design for the benefit of both the audience and the environment.

Elocution Competition

The participating students should choose one of the following sub-topics as their topic for the Elocution Competition

Theme: Family and Community

Faith and community in Islam are deeply intertwined, as faith guides individuals in their relationship with Allah and their interactions within the Muslim community. The strength of the Muslim community lies in its unity, support system, and shared values, all grounded in a common faith. This imperative theme was incorporated to depict and showcase the essential importance of understanding the connection of faith and community with the Holy Quran and the Sunnah.

A beautiful ayah that depicts the favours of Allah (SWT) are mentioned in verse 12 of Surah-Al-Luqman, where Allah (SWT) conveys to Luqman the significance of being grateful.

Location

The event will be held physically on-campus at Dubai Scholars Private School. Location: Behind Lulu Hypermarket, Behind Stadium Metro Station, Al Qusais, Dubai, U.A.E.
