Elocution Competition

  • •Open to Islamic students only
  • •The Qirat Competition will consist of two rounds:
  • •Round 1 - Participants will have to recite their assigned Surah, based on their age category, in seperate assigned classrooms
  • •Round 2 - The qualifying students from the first round will be required to recite a preselected section from the Quran.
  • •The participants must recite the Surah from the beginning for a duration of 90 seconds.
  • •Participants are timed to recite for 90 seconds starting from any ayah chosen by the judge.
  • •While reciting, if a participant starts the recitation again (due to some error), then the time will not be recorded again.
